I have to stop at my locker. *As you walk to your locker you see all sorts of oddities in the hall* What in the world is this? Am I hallucinating? *Every student is transforming before your very own eyes. I don't understand why I still can't get this vision out of my head. It doesn't even feel like a memory. No. It's so much more intimate than that. And on top of that, why am I able to see everyone's name above their head? I don't care about half of the students in this school, yet I can somehow see their name. I think I really need sleep.
